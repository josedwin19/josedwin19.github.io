## The AI Revolution in Cryo-EM

Cryo-electron microscopy (cryo-EM) has become one of the most powerful techniques for determining protein structures at atomic resolution. However, the data collection and analysis process generates enormous volumes of information that require intensive processing.

### Where Does AI Come In?

Artificial intelligence is impacting cryo-EM in several key areas:

1. **Particle picking**: Deep learning algorithms can automatically classify particles in micrographs, distinguishing real proteins from ice artifacts.

2. **Optimal freezing prediction**: Trained models can predict vitrified ice quality before acquisition, saving valuable microscope time.

3. **Accelerated 3D reconstruction**: Convolutional neural networks accelerate 3D reconstruction by reducing computational noise.

4. **Conformational analysis**: Unsupervised learning enables identification of multiple conformational states from heterogeneous datasets.

### Toward an Integrated Workflow

The future points to fully automated pipelines where AI assists at every stage: from sample evaluation in the microscope, to final structure refinement.
