I work in the Cryo-Electron Microscopy facility supporting high-resolution structural determination of macromolecular complexes. My activities include sample preparation, grid optimization, data acquisition, image processing, and structural interpretation of protein assemblies.

Research Interests

Single-particle analysis, sample optimization, and high-resolution structure determination.

Macromolecular Assemblies

Filament formation, enzymatic activation mechanisms, and regulatory complexes.

Biophysical Characterization

Integration of structural biology with biochemical and quantitative data analysis.
